Movie Magic vs Real Science

Movie Weebo (1997)

  • Anti-gravity hover (no visible propulsion)
  • True free-floating holograms
  • Human-like emotions and consciousness
  • Unlimited battery life
  • Compact size (volleyball-sized)
  • Advanced object manipulation
  • Silent operation

Real Tech (2024)

  • Quadcopter drones with visible propellers
  • Pepper's Ghost illusions & AR projections
  • LLM personality simulations (no true consciousness)
  • 10-30 minute battery life with current tech
  • Possible with Raspberry Pi and compact components
  • Limited manipulation (grippers, simple arms)
  • Propeller noise (40-80 dB)

Required Technology Stack

Flight System

Micro drones, quadcopters, or ducted fans for hover capability.

AI Processing

Raspberry Pi 5, NVIDIA Jetson, or smartphone chip for AI tasks.

Display System

Pico projectors, OLED screens, or holographic foil for visuals.

Power Supply

LiPo batteries, wireless charging, or supercapacitors for energy.

Manipulation

Micro servos, robotic grippers, or telescoping arms.

Sensors

Cameras, microphones, LIDAR, and IMU for environmental awareness.
